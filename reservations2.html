Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your • name • contact information • computer or network.

We collect your personal information in order to reserve a dining booking for you or to contact you to confirm or change a reservation. Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter contact details, we'll be unable to reserve a dining booking for you.

We keep your information safe by storying it in encrypted files and only allowing certain staff to access it.

We keep people’s personal information for 12 months, as which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all digital information.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
